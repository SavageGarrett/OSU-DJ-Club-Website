Join DJ Club

DJ Club is open to members of all skill levels, and no prior experienced is necessary. We welcome experienced and established DJs as well.

Members new to DJing can expect to meet experienced DJs who can help teach you the ropes, perform at live events, and build your skills.Experienced DJs can expect to network with other campus-area and Columbus-area DJs, share their knowledge with others, and further advance their career in DJing.

We encourage you to join our GroupMe to learn more and shoot us a message if you’re interested in joining.
